Corporate Services

GVC Advisory Services Ltd. provides its clients with a variety of advisory services that include: developing turnaround plans, devising restructuring and divestiture strategies, and providing strategic acquisition and growth plans. Many of our clients also may benefit from debt restructuring and preparation for accessing debt and/or equity funding from the capital markets.

Clients may elect to retain us for analysis and the development of an action plan for an individual area of their operations. They may also retain us to make a global analysis and to provide them with recommendations for their overall operations.

The GVC Advisory Services Ltd. professional team will visit the client and conduct an extensive review of all existing operational procedures and controls. Upon completion of its review, GVC Advisory Services Ltd. will present its findings to management along with the implementation strategies needed to fulfill the client's mandate. The review process may include the following:

Operations:

  • Cost controls
  • Cost avoidance
  • Strategic partnerships
  • Re-engineering (product, product flow, strategy)
  • Inventory controls
  • Receivables controls
  • Internal communications, operations monitoring and information networking
  • Management and employee performance evaluations

Finance:

  • Financial modeling
  • Benchmarking
  • Cash flow projections
  • Budget and forecast preparation
  • Variance analysis
  • Internal controls
  • Financing requirements

Strategies:

  • Business plan review and execution
  • Competitive analysis
  • Merger/acquisition strategy
  • Formation of a viable Board of Directors and Advisory Board
  • Information systems analysis
  • Company structure/organization
  • Strategic alliances

Marketing:

  • Pricing analysis
  • Distribution analysis
  • Strategic partnerships and marketing plan

Strategic Acquisition & Growth Planning

GVC's professional team will assist the client in determining if revenue growth is optimally achieved via acquisition, generic expansion of current capabilities, or if a no-growth policy should be instituted while operations are stabilized. The evaluation process will include a thorough financial and operational analysis of the target acquisition, the target market, and target valuation.

Debt Restructuring & Refinancing

Following a strategic evaluation of the client's current and projected financial situation, a restructuring of short and long-term debt or the placement of new debt may be appropriate. Projects could entail:

  • Negotiated workouts
  • Refinancing
  • Releases
  • Long-term payment plans of all liabilities

Once a client is "credit worthy" and is prepared for presentation to the capital markets for refinancing or initial funding, GVC can facilitate funding through its extensive network of lenders and investors, including specialists in the following areas:

  • Senior debt
  • Mezzanine debt
  • Bridge financing
  • Buyout funds
  • Private equity groups
  • Traunch B
