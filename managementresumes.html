Management Resumes

GVC Advisory Services Ltd.

Melvin I. Gilbert - President and CEO

Melvin Gilbert is the President and CEO of GVC Advisory Services Ltd. Mr. Gilbert has been a Financial Consultant for over thirty years and has had a high degree of success in raising debt and equity for many types of companies, as well as doing an extensive amount of Merger and Acquisition work. He also has served as a crisis advisor assisting troubled companies to restructure and refinance. Prior to establishing GVC Advisory Services Ltd., Mr. Gilbert was the Executive Vice President of CFC Investments, LLC. CFC was a Financial Consulting firm and buyer of Consumer Paper. Mr. Gilbert maintains an extensive network in the debt and equity sector and strong relationships with Equity Funds and Lenders.

Ernie Vicentina - Chief Financial Officer

As the CFO of GVC Advisory Services Ltd., Mr. Vicentina participates and engages in the due diligence screening of projects, financial modeling, financing strategies, and negotiating term sheets from financial sources. Mr. Vicentina's professional career has been spent in the financial and operational working environments of companies. Prior to GVC Advisory Services Ltd., Mr. Vicentina was the CFO for CFC Investments, LLC where Mr. Vicentina developed control guidelines, improved underwriting policies, and implemented a cash flow planning program. Mr. Vicentina has over twenty years experience working in the different stages of business from start-ups to mature settings. Mr. Vicentina has been involved with companies in a wide range of industries that includes manufacturing, retail, transportation, real estate, services, and finance.
